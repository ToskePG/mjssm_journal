Montenegrin Journal of Sports Science and Medicine are always seeking qualified, dedicated reviewers to assist with the manuscript review process. The vitality of the journals program depends on the willingness of volunteers to serve in this important role.

Why become a reviewer?

  • Include it on your curriculum vitae;
  • Get an insider's knowledge of the peer-review process;
  • Read cutting edge research before anyone else;
  • Contribute further to your field;
  • Work with other editors and associate editors;
  • Get recognized in the last issue each year.

How do I become a reviewer?

If you have a will to become a reviewer, send a request to Journal Office accompanied with motivation statement.

Improve your chances of being invited to review a manuscript by specifying and explaining the areas of your expertise and keywords fields.

Keep your data up to date with current address information and areas of expertise by sending updates to the Journal Office from time to time.
